About Me

I am a Master's student in Human Systems Engineering at Arizona State University and a Mastercard Foundation Scholar. My work focuses on Human-AI collaboration, responsible AI, educational technology, human factors engineering, and data analytics.

Before pursuing graduate studies, I worked as an educator and technology trainer in Ghana. Today, I combine my background in education, research, and technology to design and evaluate systems that improve human performance, learning, and collaboration.

AI Emotional Valence and Team Performance

Master's thesis research investigating AI emotional tone, communication sentiment and collaborative performance in Human-AI teams.

Critical Code Reviewer (CCR) Framework

Contributed to the development of the Critical Code Reviewer (CCR) framework, a responsible AI tool designed to help software developers identify ethical, fairness, accessibility, and trust-related concerns during code review.

Conducted literature reviews, contributed to framework design, and participated in interdisciplinary collaboration with researchers and software engineers.

Student Success Prediction Using Machine Learning

Human-Autonomy Teaming Research
